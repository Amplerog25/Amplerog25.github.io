Here is a few facts about the pugs you might like to learn:

The earliest records of the Pug come from China, where Pugs were pets in Tibetan Buddhist monasteries.

As you can see from image above.Originally, pugs had long legs, longer noses, straight tails and a slim build. However, they've been bred over time to have shorter legs, flatter noses, curly tails and a stockier build.
