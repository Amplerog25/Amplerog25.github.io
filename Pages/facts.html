Here is a few facts about the pugs you might like to learn:

The earliest records of the Pug come from China, where Pugs were pets in Tibetan Buddhist monasteries.

As you can see from the image above. Originally pugs had long legs, longer noses,and a slim build. However, they've been bred over time to have shorter legs, flatter noses, curly tails and a stockier build. The paiting name “A favourite Pug” painted by Henry Bernard Chalon was made in 1802. It shows how selective breeding had changed the pug as we know them today.
